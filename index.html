Frequently Asked Questions

We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow strict security protocols. Your financial data is processed securely and never shared with third parties. All data transmission is encrypted, and we comply with industry standards including SOC 2 and GDPR.

What financial systems does Asimply integrate with? ▼

Asimply integrates with major accounting software like QuickBooks, Xero, and Sage, as well as banking institutions and payment processors. Our API-first approach allows for custom integrations with your existing financial stack.

Our AI models achieve 99.9% accuracy in financial forecasting based on historical data analysis. The more data you provide, the more accurate our predictions become. We continuously refine our algorithms to improve accuracy over time.
